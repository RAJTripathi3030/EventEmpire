## Usage Instructions

1. Replace GIF imports in Home.js with these video URLs
2. Use HTML5 `<video>` element with attributes:
   - `autoPlay`
   - `loop`
   - `muted`
   - `playsInline`
3. Add fallback poster images for browsers that don't support video
